Hello, I'm Pragya

I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, education, and healthcare, with a focus on responsible real-world use.

My role focuses on designing and delivering training for educators and professional services colleagues, including prompt engineering, AI-assisted workflows, accessibility-focused use cases, and applied guidance for teaching, learning, and academic work. I am particularly interested in helping people move from curiosity to confident, thoughtful use of AI in their everyday practice.

Alongside my education work, I have a strong interest in AI applications in healthcare and mental health, especially projects that combine data, human-centred design, and real-world impact. I am currently building a personal project focused on an AI and mental health application. Follow my AI Mental Health Project Updates. My background spans artificial intelligence, psychology, and data analysis, and I am keen to collaborate across disciplines where these areas meet.

If you are an educator, learning designer, or researcher exploring how generative AI can be used responsibly in education, or thinking about cross-sector AI applications, I am always happy to connect and exchange ideas.
