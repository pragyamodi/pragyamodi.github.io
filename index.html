Discover My Journey

Hello, I'm Pragya

As a masters student in Artificial Intelligence, my journey is driven by a passion for leveraging technology to improve healthcare. While my primary focus is on mental health, I'm deeply interested in the broader applications of AI across various health domains, including biotech and personalized medicine.

My interdisciplinary background in Computer Science and Psychology has given me a unique perspective on the challenges and opportunities in health tech. I'm constantly exploring innovative ways to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical healthcare solutions.

When I'm not immersed in research or coding, I find inspiration in nature and solo travel. These experiences not only help me maintain my own well-being but also fuel creative approaches to my work in AI and health technology.
